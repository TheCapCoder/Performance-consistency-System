FAQ – Competitive Gaming Consistency

Quick answers to common questions before diving deeper into execution consistency systems.

Is this coaching?

No. This is not 1-on-1 coaching or advice based on rank. It’s a structured system designed to stabilize execution.

Does this work for my game?

The system applies to competitive ranked environments: Valorant, League of Legends, CS, Rocket League, and similar games.

Do I need to be a high-rank player?

No. Execution instability affects players at every level. The system focuses on consistency, not rank.

How long does it take to use?

The system is designed to be used during real ranked sessions, not as an extra workload.
